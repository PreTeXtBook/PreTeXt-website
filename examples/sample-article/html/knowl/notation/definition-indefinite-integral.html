Suppose that \(\frac{d}{dx}F(x)=f(x)\text{.}\) Then the indefinite integral of \(f(x)\) is \(F(x)\) and is written as
\begin{equation*} \int\,f(x)\,dx=F(x)\text{.} \end{equation*}
