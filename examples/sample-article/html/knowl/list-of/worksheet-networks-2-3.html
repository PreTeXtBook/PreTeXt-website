<theorem>
    <title> Ohms Law </title>
    <p> The current through a resistor is proportional to the ratio of the <em> Voltage </em> to the <em> Resistance </em>
        <me> I = \frac{V}{R} </me>
        Or for our purposes
        <me> I R = V </me>
    </p>
</theorem>
The current through a resistor is proportional to the ratio of the Voltage to the Resistance
\begin{equation*} I = \frac{V}{R} \end{equation*}
Or for our purposes
\begin{equation*} I R = V \end{equation*}
