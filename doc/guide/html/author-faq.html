    12. Why are theorems, definitions, examples, remarks, etc.Â all numbered using the same counter?

    16. Why not LaTeX? Why PreTeXt?
      TeX was first released in 1978. There was no Internet, no HTML, no Unicode, and no YouTube. There have been many attempts to convert TeX/LaTeX to more modern document formats. They are not hard to findâ€”none is satisfactory. We know because we have spent many years trying to adapt them to our purposes.
      ðŸ”—

LaTeX allows authors enough freedom that it is impossible to accurately discern intent in a totally automated way.

The result is automated conversions to many useful output formats with no extra effort from the author, including future conversions to formats not yet imagined. And XSL, once understood, is a robust and powerful tool for the sorts of text-manipulation tasks necessary.
