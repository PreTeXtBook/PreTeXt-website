The xsl subdirectory of the PreTeXt distribution contains a variety of XSL stylesheets, which I will also refer to as converters or conversions . The ones that you will use as an author all have filenames of the form xsl/pretext-XXX.xsl, where XXX is some indication of the output produced. Conversions to LaTeXor HTML output are the two most mature converters.
