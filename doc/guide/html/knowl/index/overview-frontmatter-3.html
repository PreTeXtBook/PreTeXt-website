In the beginning of your <book> or <article> you can have a <frontmatter> element that contains various items that would precede your first <chapter> or <section> (respectively). Possibilities include <bibinfo> (to hold bibliographic information about your document), <titlepage>, <colophon>, <biography>, <abstract>, <dedication>, <acknowledgement>, <foreword>, and <preface>. Some of these may be duplicated (e.g., several prefaces for multiple editions), many of these items are restricted to books (e.g., a foreword), and some items are restricted to articles (e.g., an abstract).
