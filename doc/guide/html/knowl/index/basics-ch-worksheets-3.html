A <worksheet> is a specialized division that can be a child of most divisions and can contain most PreTeXt tags. The general idea, however, would be to assemble a sequence of <exercise>s or project-like elements with some interspersed <p>, <figure>, and <sidebyside>. One of the few times that PreTeXt allows you to manipulate page layout in terms of giving blank space and specifying page breaks is in the <worksheet> tag. A <worksheet> is also the only place you can put an <exercise> inside a <sidebyside>.
